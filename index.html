The Heritage of UB Perfumes

Rooted in centuries-old traditions of perfumery, UB Perfumes brings you the purest essences sourced from around the globe. Our attars are distilled using traditional methods to preserve the soul of the botanicals, ensuring a fragrance that is deeply personal and long-lasting.

Whether you seek the deep, woody resonance of Oudh or the delicate, fleeting whisper of fresh florals, our collection is designed for the connoisseur who understands that a scent is an invisible signature.
